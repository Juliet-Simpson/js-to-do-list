Things To Do

How To Use

This app enables you to keep everything you must remember to do organised. You can choose one of 4 interface themes using the button bar at the top of the screen.
Create separate named lists of 'Things To Do' (or anything you like!) and then you can open each list individually to review and edit it.
Before adding anything to do, a list title must be showing. This can be created entering text in the New List input box and pressing enter or selecting a list name from previously named lists under the 'Current Lists' heading. All the to do items that were already on that list will then also show in the main part of the page.

To do items can then be added to the 'Add Something To Do' text input field.

UrgentUrgent items to do can be highlighted in red by clicking the exclamation icon to the right of the item and deselected as non-urgent by clicking the icon a second time.

Unchecked/Checked 'Done' items can be checked by clicking the circle to its left. The text of a checked item will also show a line through. A second click on the circle will uncheck the item.

Trash Delete a to do item by clicking on this icon.

Start a new list using this button, your previous list will be saved.

Delete a list by first selecting its name under Current Lists and then hitting this button.
